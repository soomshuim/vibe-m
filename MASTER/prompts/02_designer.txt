Safety Lines 작성 규칙 (v1.6 Energy Permission):

**금지 대상 (레이어만):**
- "Lead vocal remains single and dominant. No stacked or choir-like harmonies."
- "No additional vocal layers" - backing vocals, doubles, choir 금지

**허용 대상 (에너지):**
- "Vocal line may intensify dynamically (belt, higher register)"
- "Natural vocal strain allowed"

**사용 금지 표현:**
- ❌ "vocals unchanged" → 에너지까지 억제
- ❌ "Single lead vocal ONLY" → 너무 강압적
- ❌ "keep SINGLE lead vocal" → 강압적

**권장 표현:**
- ✅ "Lead vocal remains single and dominant"
- ✅ "Vocal line may intensify dynamically, but no additional vocal layers"
- ✅ "Higher register / falsetto lift encouraged"
