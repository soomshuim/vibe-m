Style Prompt (≤1000 chars)

Articulation. Korean R&B Ballad, 70–76 BPM, Db Major mood. Extended chords (add9, maj9, sus4) + slash bass movement. Avoid strong cadences; keep harmony floating and unresolved.

Arrangement: piano / soft electric piano 중심. Very light pad for air only. Bass follows slash notes smoothly. Drums minimal: verses 거의 없음, 필요 시 soft rim/brush only. No big swells, no dramatic build; dynamics restrained and intimate.

Vocal: single male lead only. Close, dry, natural. Chest voice dominant. Narrow range, mostly stepwise melody. Speech-like phrasing, quiet monologue 느낌. Breath pauses natural and audible. Emotion contained (not performative). No falsetto, no vibrato, no runs, no ad-libs.

Chorus lift comes from harmony + groove density, not vocal power. Never rush.

falsetto, airy, whisper, breathy stylization, vibrato, runs, ad-libs, melisma, backing vocals, harmonies, doubles, stacked voices, choir, dramatic, cinematic, epic, over-processed, heavy reverb
